Meeting notes without handing meetings to someone else's cloud.

Distavo is the thin native Mac layer around your own transcription and summarisation setup. It watches, converts, calls your configured endpoints, validates, and writes notes locally.

Watch

Use the folder you already sync.

Point Distavo at a local, iCloud Drive, or Google Drive folder. When a recording finishes syncing, Distavo processes it once.

Transcribe

Send audio only where you decide.

AVFoundation converts media locally, then Distavo uploads the WAV to the WhisperX endpoint you configured.

Summarise

Write Markdown notes with your model.

The cleaned transcript goes to your Ollama server, then Distavo writes a structured note to your notes folder.

Recordings are useful. They are also regulated.

Distavo can turn recordings into notes, but it cannot decide whether a meeting should be recorded, shared, stored, or used as evidence. Treat recording law, privacy law, workplace rules, and court procedure as separate questions.

Not legal advice. Check the rules where you and the other participants are located before recording sensitive calls, meetings, medical notes, interviews, HR conversations, or anything you may later want to rely on formally.
Consent

Permission rules vary by place.

Some places allow one participant to record. Others require everyone on the call or in the room to agree. Cross-border calls can make this more complex.

Purpose

Making a note is not the same as publishing it.

Even when recording is allowed, later transcription, sharing, workplace use, or data retention may need a lawful basis, notice, policy, or explicit consent.

Evidence

Court or tribunal use is a separate hurdle.

A recording that was lawful to make may still be challenged, excluded, limited, or require permission before it can be used in a trial, grievance, or regulated process.

Free and open tools that pair well with Distavo.

Distavo watches a folder, so any recorder that can save or export audio into your watched folder can fit the workflow. Prefer tools that are open source, export ordinary audio files, and work offline when possible.

Android · F-Droid

Notely Voice

GPLv3 notes and AI voice transcription app. Good when you want voice notes, imported-audio transcription, export, and phone-first capture before moving files to your Distavo folder.

Android · F-Droid

Fossify Voice Recorder

Open-source, ad-free recorder for simple voice capture. Useful when you want a minimal phone recorder and will let Distavo do the transcription and summarisation later.

Android · F-Droid

Sound Recorder Plus

Basic GPLv3 sound recorder with straightforward recording management and high-quality recording defaults.

Android · F-Droid

Audio Recorder

Apache-licensed recorder that can create M4A or WAV files, tune sample rate and bitrate, and share or import recordings.

macOS · Windows · Linux

OBS Studio

Free, open-source option for screen, window, microphone, and system-audio capture when you need more than a voice memo.

macOS · Windows · Linux

Audacity

Free, open-source audio recorder and editor for cleaning, trimming, converting, or exporting recordings before Distavo processes them.

Native settings. No local web panel.

The App Store build is sandboxed and uses security-scoped bookmarks for folders. Direct and Setapp builds keep the same core pipeline with edition-specific distribution behavior.

Configure server URLs, models, speaker options, folders, and local fallback from the Mac settings window. The app has no telemetry and no phone-home.
