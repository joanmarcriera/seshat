# Trademark / Name-Availability Notes — "Distavo" (macOS app)

**Subject:** A macOS menu-bar app named **Distavo** that turns meeting recordings into Markdown
notes via the user's own WhisperX + Ollama servers (local-first, bring-your-own-server).

**Status:** Informal, evidence-based screening (June 2026) — **not legal advice** and **not a formal
clearance search.** Complete the checklist in §4 before *filing* a mark.

---

## 1. Why the name is "Distavo"

**Distavo is a coined word**, evoking **distil / distillation** — the product distils a raw recording
into a clean, structured note. It is not a dictionary word in English (incidental only: it is the
Italian imperfect of *distare*, "I was distant" — obscure, and not a word in Catalan/Spanish).
Being coined and arbitrary gives it strong trademark distinctiveness.

**Name lineage** (kept for provenance / good-faith adoption evidence):

1. **Scribed** — dropped: sat inside the crowded "scribe / transcription" cluster, with a directly
   competing commercial product under a near-identical name and a near-homophone of a registered mark.
2. **Seshat** (Egyptian goddess of writing/record-keeping) — dropped: the exact name **"Seshat" was
   already taken as an app name on the Mac App Store** (App Store Connect rejected it), and the
   broader name carried collision risk.
3. **Distavo** — adopted after the screening below. Bundle IDs: `uk.co.riera.distavo` /
   `uk.co.riera.distavo-setapp`.

## 2. Screening findings on "Distavo" (June 2026)

Evidence-based screening across registers, app stores, domains, and the open web:

- **USPTO** (`tmsearch.uspto.gov`, authoritative): **0 records** for DISTAVO / DISTAVIO / DISTAV, any
  class — corroborated by Justia and Trademarkia ("may be available").
- **EUIPO** (eSearch Plus + production DB): **0 records** for DISTAVO in any class.
- **UKIPO** (via TMview mirror; the live portal is bot-gated): **0 records**.
- **Only near-match anywhere:** `DISTAVIEW` — EUTM 001401041, word mark, Class 9 (IT hardware), owner
  Avocent Huntsville LLC — **EXPIRED/DEAD since 2009**. Different word, dead ~17 years, hardware not
  software. **No obstacle.**
- **App Store** (Mac + iOS, iTunes Search API): no app named "Distavo"; no near matches.
- **Web / common-law:** no company, product, startup, or app named Distavo (only an Italian verb form
  and a couple of tiny personal social handles). Nothing loads on `distavo.com/.io/.app`.
- **Domains:** `distavo.com` secured (Cloudflare). `distavo.app` / `distavo.io` were available at
  standard price at time of screening.

**Verdict:** materially cleaner than the prior names — no live or pending mark found in US/UK/EU
(incl. Class 9 software / Class 42 SaaS), no competing product, and a coined/distinctive term. Strong
position, but the residual gaps in §3 mean this is screening, **not** a formal clearance opinion.

## 3. Residual gaps (the ~5–8% not machine-verifiable)

- **WIPO Madrid** (Global Brand DB / Madrid Monitor) and the **UKIPO live portal** were CAPTCHA/bot
  gated during automated screening — verified via mirrors (TMview), which can lag the live register.
- This was an **identical-string knockout screen**, not an examiner-grade phonetic/conceptual search.
- Non-Madrid national offices (e.g. China CNIPA, Brazil INPI) were out of scope.

## 4. Before *filing* a trademark — checklist

Distavo is cleared to **adopt and use**. Do these before *filing your own mark*:

- [ ] **Manual confirmation on live registers** — one `*DISTAVO*` wildcard pass in `tmsearch.uspto.gov`
      and EUIPO eSearch Plus (live browser), plus `branddb.wipo.int` (WIPO) and the live UKIPO portal,
      in **Class 9** (downloadable software) and **Class 42** (SaaS). A paid clearance or a trademark
      attorney (a few hundred GBP) buys an examiner-grade phonetic-neighbour scan — cheap insurance.
- [ ] **App Store Connect name** — confirm "Distavo" is accepted for the app record under
      `uk.co.riera.distavo` (re-create the App ID/app record under the new name).
- [ ] **Domain + handle sweep** — `distavo.com` secured; optionally grab `distavo.app` (~£10,
      defensive) and matching GitHub org / social handles before announcing.
- [ ] **Decide on filing** — once confirmed, a **UK IPO** filing is the cheapest home register for a
      UK-based developer; optionally **USPTO** (intent-to-use) and an **EUTM** if selling into the EU.
      File in **Class 9** and **Class 42**.
- [ ] **Document the clearance** (screenshots/dates) as evidence of good-faith adoption.
